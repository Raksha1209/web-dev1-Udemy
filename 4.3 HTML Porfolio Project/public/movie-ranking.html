The Best Series According to Raksha in 2024, so far!

Diving into the World of Booming K-Drama.

3.Doctor slump

Firstly, the leads of this drama are visually captivating, and it's refreshing to see them reunite on screen. As for the story, it centers on two doctors who find themselves at a low point in their lives. The narrative unfolds as they become each other's saviors despite initially having a love-hate relationship.

2.Wonderful World

This currently airing drama has already captured the hearts of many, with its initial episodes evoking tears from viewers. It follows the journey of a mother who tragically loses her child and seeks vengeance, leading her to imprisonment. Years later, she reunites with her family but becomes intertwined with a mysterious boy. The narrative delves into the enigma surrounding the boy's circumstances and the secrets that shroud his life.

1.Welcome To Samdalri

One of the best movies I've watched recently, the warmth conveyed by the drama still lingers. The portrayal of love between the two protagonists sets a remarkable example for this generation. The story is intricately woven; the female lead, a passionate and skilled professional photographer, is renowned for her work. However, her world shatters when rumors spread about her harassing a coworker, forcing her to return to her hometown, which she dislikes. Meanwhile, our male lead, deeply rooted in his hometown despite being offered promotions elsewhere, resides across the street. The tale unfolds from there, leaving the rest for your eyes to behold!
